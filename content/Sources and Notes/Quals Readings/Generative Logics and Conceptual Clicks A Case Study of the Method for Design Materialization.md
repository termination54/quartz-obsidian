Khaled, R.; Barr, P. Generative Logics and Conceptual Clicks: A Case Study of the Method for Design Materialization. _Design Issues_ **2023**, _39_ (1), 55–69. [https://doi.org/10.1162/desi_a_00706](https://doi.org/10.1162/desi_a_00706)

This article argues in favor of an epistemological approach to game design research, as Khaled and Barr have identified a gap in games scholarship in relation to "engagement with the materiality of making and ways of examining the design process," citing that the bulk of prior research is concerned with traditional HCI. They propose that by keeping a reflective journal via detailed version control notes that the design process will be demystified for outsiders and allow for more in-depth case studies to be generated.
