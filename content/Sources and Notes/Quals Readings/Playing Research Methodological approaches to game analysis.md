- Game Criticism, Analysis & History, 
- Games & Society, 
- Game Systems & Game Design, 
- Technical Skills, Programming & Algorithms, 
- Visual Design, 
- Audio Design, 
- Interactive Storytelling, Writing & Scripting, 
- Business of Gaming, 
- People & Process Management"
